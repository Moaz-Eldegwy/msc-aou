MSC AOU Egypt

Organizational Charter & Blueprint

Vision Statement

"To build a unified, community of student technologists across Egypt, acting as the primary talent pipeline that connects our universities with the global tech industry and drives local innovation using Microsoft technologies."

"نهدف إننا نوحّد طلاب الـ IT في مصر في مجتمع واحد، يكون حلقة وصل بين جامعاتنا وشركات التكنولوجيا في السوق، ويساعد في نشر الابتكار في مصر باستخدام تقنيات مايكروسوفت."

Mission Statement

"Our mission is to empower Egyptian university students by providing hands-on training in Microsoft technologies, fostering a national network of peers and mentors, and creating tangible pathways to career opportunities in the tech industry. We do this through practical workshops, collaborative projects, and direct engagement with industry leaders."

"مهمتنا نساعد طلاب الجامعات في مصر يتعلموا تقنيات مايكروسوفت بشكل عملي، ونتيح لهم يتواصلوا مع بعض ومع خبراء المجال، ونوفر لهم فرص حقيقية لبداية طريقهم في شغل الIT. بنعمل ده من خلال ورش عمل، ومشاريع مشتركة، وانشطة تفاعلية، وتعاون مع الناس اللي شغالة في المجال."

Core Team Member Criteria

1. Passionate & Curious

A genuine love for technology and an unstoppable desire to learn what's next.

شغوف وفضولي

يحب التكنولوجيا بجد، ودايمًا حابب يتعلم الجديد.

2. Community-First

Motivation comes from empowering others; they succeed when the community succeeds.

يهتم بالمجتمع أولًا

دافعه إنه يساعد الناس ويتأكد إن الكل بيستفيد وينجح مع بعض.

3. Proactive & Reliable

They don't wait to be told what to do, and the team can trust a task will be done.

استباقي ويعتمد عليه

ما يستناش حد يقوله يعمل إيه، ولو استلم تاسك يخلصها.

4. Growth-Oriented

Sees challenges as learning opportunities for technical and leadership skills.

يسعى للتطور

بيشوف التحديات فرص يتعلم منها ويطور نفسه ومهاراته مش باب للخلافات.

5. Resilient Problem-Solver

Stays positive, finds solutions, and helps move the team forward when things go wrong.

مرن وبيحل المشاكل

حتى لو الأمور ما مشتش كويس، بيبقى إيجابي ويدور على الحل ويساعد الفريق يتحرك لقدام.

University Students (The Members)

Who they are:

The core audience from across Egyptian universities seeking to gain skills and build their careers.

Relationship:

They are the community the club serves. Their active participation is the measure of success.

Gives:

Time, engagement, feedback, and a desire to learn.

Takes:

Practical tech skills, a strong project portfolio, career opportunities, and a peer network.

Club Volunteers (The Core Team)

Who they are:

The dedicated students who organize, lead, and run all club activities.

Relationship:

They are the engine of the club.

Gives:

Time, passion, and leadership to plan events, teach workshops, and manage partnerships.

Takes:

Invaluable leadership and project management experience, deep networking opportunities, and a standout resume.

Microsoft (The Enabler)

Who they are:

The global technology partner providing resources, legitimacy, and a technological focus.

Relationship:

The primary partner and sponsor.

Gives:

Official branding, access to programs like Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors, software credits, learning materials, and connections to experts.

Takes:

A direct talent pipeline, brand advocacy on campus, product feedback, and a future generation of developers skilled in their ecosystem.

Industry (The Hirer)

Who they are:

Local and international tech companies that need skilled talent.

Relationship:

The career destination for members.

Gives:

Sponsorship, expert speakers, mentors, real-world project ideas, and internship/job opportunities.

Takes:

Access to a curated pool of motivated and pre-skilled students, reducing hiring costs.
